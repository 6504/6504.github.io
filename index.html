CHANGE THE WORLD

We are the Falls Church Robotics Team, otherwise known as Team 6504 - JagWires, based in Falls Church, Virginia. We strive to promote STEM education to our peers and our community through various general-headers and events. We participate in multiple robotics general-headers, namely the FIRST Robotics Challenge (FRC), FIRST Tech Challenge (FTC), and VEX Robotics.

VEX Robotics

One of the beginning stepping stones into robotics, the VEX Robotics Competition is an intermediate level robotics general-header, and was the first general-header we competed in. Since our first VEX Competition in 2014, we have won several distinctions with our robots; our many, many robots.

FIRST Tech Challenge

The alternate beginning step into the world of robotics, the FIRST Tech Challenge, also known as FTC, is an intermediate level robotics general-header. We began competing in FTC in 2015, and since then we have won several awards with our robots.

View robots »

FIRST Robotics Challenge

The FIRST Robotics Challenge, also known as FRC, is the largest general-header we compete in. Aimed for high schoolers, students work with advanced machinery, large industrial tools, and computer aided design, electronics, programming, vision processing, and robotics autonomy. FRC also challenges students in other fields, such as business, film, art and animation, and marketing.
