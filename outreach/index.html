COMMUNITY OUTREACH

Besides building robots for competition, we work to spread our passion for technology and engineering. We interact with our local community to cultivate a culture of appreciation towards computers and machines, and help students grow their passion for the STEM field in various outreach events.

We are commited to helping other students and people develop an understanding of STEM. Here are some examples of how we help:

LEGO NXT Competition

Each year, we host a LEGO Mindstorms competition. Anyone can compete, and each year is a different theme. This is helpful because many local schools around us do not have NXT robots, and this competition lets people build and program their own robots, free of charge.
