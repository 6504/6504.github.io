BECOME A SPONSOR

We are ever so thankful to our sponsors and donors, who have helped transform our club into something new. Without them, we would not have been able to be a part of this wonderful community. With your money, we are able to enter competitions, buy tools, parts, and of course, food.

If you would like to donate, please follow these steps:

1. Send us an email/letter about your interest in sponsorship/dontation. (detail any specific requests, we'd be heppy to negotitate them.) In the email/letter, please include your organization/preferred name, your logo (if there is one), and a link to your website if preferred.

Send Us an Email About Sponsorship »

2. Write a check, of any donation amount and send it to us!

Checks can be sent to the following address (please make it payable to "FCHS Robotics Team"):

Falls Church High School

7521 Jaguar Trail

Falls Church, VA 22042

Based on the amount donated, donors are placed into one of three tiers:

  • Gold Tier - $1000+

  • Green Tier - $500+

  • White Tier - All other amounts

Also, there are benefits based on which rank the donor is in:

  • Gold Tier ($1000+) - Logo on all Robots (VEX, FTC, FRC), on Team Shirt, on our website, and all other promotional items

  • Green Tier ($500+) - Logo on 1 robot of your choosing, on Team Shirt, on our website, and all promotional items

  • White Tier (All other amounts) - Logo on Website
