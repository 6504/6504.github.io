MEET THE TEAM

We are thankful to have so many wonderful students and mentors on our team to push our once-small club to even greater heights. In recognition of our students and coaches alike, we have a complete profile of our current and past members who have all built our team to where it is today.

MENTORS

Ralph Albrecht

Parent Volunteer/Mentor

Ralph Albrecht is a patent attorney who founded Albrecht Law Firm. He is also the head coach and a parent volunteer of the Falls Church Robotics Team.

John Bertha

Team Sponsor/Mentor

John Bertha is the teacher-sponsor of the team and is currently a teacher at Falls Church. He is a coach of the Falls Church Robotics Team.

Sokhom Kith

Parent Volunteer/Mentor

Sohkom Kith is an engineer at the Naval Research Labs in Washington D.C. He is also a coach and parent volunteer of the Falls Church Robotics team.

Joshua Booth

Programming Mentor

Joshua Booth is a software developer at CSRA. His primary role is the programming mentor of the team, however, he assists other subdivisions as well.

Jeff Kelbick

Mentor

In addition to volunteering at various FIRST events, Jeff Kelbick is a mentor of the team and helps with paperwork and entering the team into events.

Anhtuan Nguyen

Alumni Mentor

One of the founding presidents of the Falls Church Robotics Team, Anhtuan Nguyen is a returning Alumni Mentor who is majoring in Mechanical Engineering at George Mason Univeristy.
